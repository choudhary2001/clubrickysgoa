Refund Policy

1. Event Cancellation by Club Ricky's Goa

If an event is cancelled by Club Ricky's Goa, you will receive a full refund of the ticket price within 7-10 business days.

2. Cancellation by Customer

Refund Schedule:

  • 7+ days before event: 100% refund
  • 3-6 days before event: 50% refund
  • 0-2 days before event: No refund

3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team
  2. Provide your booking reference
  3. State your reason for cancellation
  4. Choose your preferred refund method

4. Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days, depending on your payment method and bank.
