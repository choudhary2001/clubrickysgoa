Our History

It all started in 1971 when Tito Henry De Souza opened a small beachfront restaurant in Baga, unknowingly laying the foundation for Goa's most legendary nightlife empire. What began as a cozy dining spot soon evolved into the iconic Tito's Group, turning Tito's Lane into the party capital of India.

Club Ricky's was introduced as a complete experience‚Äîoffering a vibrant mix of Goan and Indian cuisine, along with an extensive drink menu. By night, it transforms into an electrifying hotspot with high-energy beats, a vibrant dance floor, and a beachside pool vibe that keeps the party alive well into the night.

With its 2024 Martin Audio upgrade, Club Ricky's isn't just keeping up ~ it's setting the standard. If you're looking for great food, drinks, and a night that feels like a festival, Club Ricky's is where you need to be.

Our Mission

At Club Ricky's Goa, our mission is to deliver an unparalleled nightlife experience that embodies the vibrant, energetic spirit of Goa.

  • Excellence: From the quality of our services to the unforgettable moments we create, we strive for excellence in everything we do.
  • Hospitality: We believe in the heart of Goan hospitality ~ welcoming our guests with warmth, friendliness, and genuine care, making sure everyone feels right at home.
  • Innovation: We're all about staying ahead of the curve, bringing new and exciting experiences that keep our patrons coming back for more.

By staying true to these values, Club Ricky's aims to be the ultimate destination for music, culture, and entertainment, offering an experience that's as unforgettable as Goa itself.
